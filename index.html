Powerfull Success Mantra - The Four Pillars of Diabetes Reversal

Diabetes Reversal and related disorders focuses on deeper understanding of habits and their changes gradually step by step in systematic manner aling with Indian traditional detox/Panchakarma procedure. Our Diabetic Reversal program is based on four important pillar as per Ayurveda that include:

Diet

There are total four phase of diet Depending upon the Body Constitution i.e Vata,Pitta and Kapha and on the basis of Individual BMI( Body mass index)

  • Basic phase diet – The basic diet is for Reducing Acidic condition of body / inflammation of body ,Reduces blood sugar level and initiate body fat and weight loss.
  • Radical phase diet – This is the phase to Detoxify ,Repair ,Rehydrate ,Rejuvenate the body cell and speed up burning fat and weight loss.
  • Progressive phase diet – This is the phase to support intensive weight training exercise to develop stamina and strength.
  • Maintenance phase diet - This is a liberty diet to maintain healthy weight ( fat vs muscle mass ratio).

Exercise

BTD exercise protocol is in four phase with clear objective for each phase

  • The Basic Exercise phase is for activating all muscle fiber, increases concentration and memory , Reduces acidic and inflammation ,Reduces Blood sugar level and initiate body fat and weight loss.
  • Accelerating Exercise phase is to support the Radical diet phase to detoxify, repair and initiate muscle building.
  • Intensive Exercise Phase is complete transformation of the body through the blend of strength, stamina, yoga and marathon based practices.
  • The fourth maintenance phase is for maintaining a healthy weight( fat vs muscle mass ratio).

Detox/Panchkarma

Panchakarma is Cleansing and Detoxification process which are effective on both body and mind. In simple, body and mind get revitalized and rejuvenated from stress and strain of everyday life with Ayurvedic panchakarma treatment. Panchakarma treatment are the fivefold Ayurvedic cleansing and detoxification therapies which get to the root cause of Diabetes and eliminate deep seated toxins (ama) from the body and maintain equilibrium of doshas .Apart from providing curative line of treatment ,Ayurveda panchakarma helps in promotion of healthy and prevention of further other Diseases.
